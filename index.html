About Us

Who We Are

Five Star Group LLC is a property management company that sells direct-to-consumer gift certificates and optional in-room add-on packages for use at properties we manage. Our gift certificates are redeemable only for accommodations and related services provided by our company.

We also sell property-stay enhancements such as welcome baskets, firewood bundles, candles, snack packages, celebration packages, and other hospitality add-ons delivered directly to guests at check-in or during their stay.

We do not sell third-party gift cards, open-loop prepaid products, reloadable stored-value products, or payment services for other businesses.

Policies

Our Policies

Clear and fair policies for all customers.

Refund Policy

Gift certificates may be refunded within 30 days of purchase if unused. In-room add-on packages may be cancelled for a full refund up to 48 hours before the scheduled check-in date. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Delivery & Fulfillment

Digital gift certificates are delivered by email, typically within minutes of purchase. In-room packages and hospitality add-ons are delivered on-site to guests at check-in or during their stay at our managed properties.

Customer Verification

All orders go through standard online checkout with payment verification, fraud screening, reservation matching, and manual review when needed. We do not sell to resellers or third-party distributors.

Important Disclosures

Five Star Group LLC does not sell third-party gift cards, open-loop prepaid products, reloadable stored-value products, or payment services for other businesses. All gift certificates sold are first-party products, valid only for stays, services, and items at properties managed by Five Star Group LLC. Our products are sold directly to individual end customers.
